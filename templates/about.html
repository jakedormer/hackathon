Vestem was set up in 2020 by two friends Jake and Josh as a one-stop-shop to find clothing that you'll love. We were sick and tired of searching hours for clothes online only to be disappointed when they were delivered. This was a problem that we couldn't find a solution for and so we created Vestem as the destination to find clothes that fit, from brands that care.
